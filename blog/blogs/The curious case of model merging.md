Recently, we have seen a frenzy in the LLM community related to model merging, a method that combines multiple LLMs into a single one. It caught my eye on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/maximelabonne/status/1747350120067154227). Maxime Labonne's [blog post](https://towardsdatascience.com/merge-large-language-models-with-mergekit-2118fb392b54) got me up and running on merging models. 


Omer Sanseviero has a [collection on Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/collections/osanseviero/model-merging-65097893623330a3a51ead66) containing papers that historicize this technique.
